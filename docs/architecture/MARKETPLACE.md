# Marketplace

The marketplace has a public discovery surface and tenant-specific operational workflows.

Provider profiles, services, and products are discoverable from the public schema. Owner tenants can initiate inquiries, quote requests, and service contracts through explicit marketplace workflows.

Provider tenants manage their own offers and operational fulfillment. Owner tenants never grant providers direct schema access as part of marketplace engagement.

Future marketplace models include `MarketplaceService`, `MarketplaceProduct`, `QuoteRequest`, `ServiceContract`, `MarketplaceReview`, and `MarketplaceInquiry`.
